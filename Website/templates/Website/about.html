Our Vision

To become the most competitive and innovative motorsport team in Africa, creating a legacy that inspires future generations of African racing talent and establishes the continent as a powerhouse in global motorsport.

Our Mission

To discover, develop, and showcase African racing talent through world-class training, cutting-edge technology, and competitive racing across all motorsport disciplines, while promoting sustainable and innovative practices in motorsport.
